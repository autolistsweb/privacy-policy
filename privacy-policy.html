Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Autolist ("we", "our", or "us") operates an application that connects to your eBay account to help you list and manage items for sale. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Access

Our application accesses your eBay account only to:

  • Create and manage inventory items
  • Create and publish listings on your behalf
  • Retrieve order and sales information

We do not store, share, or sell your personal data, eBay credentials, or listing information to any third party.

3. Authentication & Tokens

We use eBay's OAuth 2.0 authentication system. Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored securely on your local machine and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Data Retention

All data processed by our application remains within your eBay account and your local environment. We do not maintain a separate database of your information.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke our application's access to your eBay account at any time via your eBay Account Settings.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@autolistsweb.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
